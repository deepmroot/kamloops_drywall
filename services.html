Our Drywall Services

Comprehensive drywall solutions for residential and commercial projects in the Kamloops area. We combine skilled craftsmanship with efficient techniques to deliver results that last.

Drywall Installation

Whether it's a new build, an extension, or a simple partition wall, we provide professional board hanging services. We ensure all boards are properly measured, cut, and fastened to minimize seams and create a solid foundation for finishing.

  • ✓ Residential New Construction
  • ✓ Commercial Tenant Improvements
  • ✓ Additions & Renovations

Taping & Mudding

The finish is where the magic happens. Our taping and mudding process involves multiple coats, meticulously sanded to a smooth finish. We offer standard Level 4 finishes and premium Level 5 finishes for high-sheen paint applications.

  • ✓ Seamless Joint Finishing
  • ✓ Internal & External Corner Beads
  • ✓ Sanding & Dust Management

Basement Finishing

Transform your unfinished basement into a functional living space. We specialize in complete drywall solutions for basement suites, home theaters, and extra bedrooms.

  • ✓ Moisture-Resistant Board Options
  • ✓ Soundproofing Solutions
  • ✓ Full Completion from Board to Sand
